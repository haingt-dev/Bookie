# Theme: Bản Sắc (Identity)

> Concepts về cách bản sắc cá nhân hình thành, thay đổi, và ảnh hưởng đến hành vi.

## Concepts

### Identity-based change
- **Source**: Atomic Habits (James Clear)
- **Position**: Most effective change = focus on who you want to BECOME, not what you want to ACHIEVE. "I don't smoke" (identity) vs "I'm trying to quit" (still a smoker). Every action = a vote for the type of person you wish to become.
- **Nuance**: The book's greatest insight. Reframes behavior as identity expression rather than willpower battle. More powerful than any formula — fundamentally changes self-narrative.

### Small wins as identity proof
- **Source**: Atomic Habits (James Clear)
- **Position**: Prove new identity through small wins. "Người không bao giờ bỏ tập" = just show up at gym for 5 minutes. Accumulation of small votes builds identity gradually.
- **Nuance**: Works when identity goal is clear. Fails when person stacks multiple borrowed identities (dậy 5h + gym + coding + reading) without personal alignment — common trap for Vietnamese youth chasing self-improvement trends.
