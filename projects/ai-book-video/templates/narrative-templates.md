# Narrative Templates

> 5 templates cho Bookie book videos. Mỗi template được kích hoạt bởi trạng thái của Knowledge Vault.
> Dùng trong `/create-storyboard` để chọn cấu trúc narrative phù hợp nhất.

---

## Template 1: Contrarian Analysis

**Trigger**: Sách nổi tiếng, nhiều video tóm tắt trên YouTube VN. Gap rõ ràng cho góc criticism/nuance.

**Vault requirement**: 1+ book in library.md. Competitive analysis confirms gap.

**Scene Structure**:

| # | Label | Purpose | Pace |
| - | ----- | ------- | ---- |
| 1 | HOOK | Challenge assumption — câu nói/con số quen thuộc bị đặt dấu hỏi | slow |
| 2 | CONTEXT | Frame as nuance, not hate. Establish credibility + fairness | normal |
| 3 | REVEAL 1 | First blind spot (dễ hiểu nhất, warm up audience) | normal |
| 4 | EVIDENCE | Real-world proof supporting reveal 1 | normal |
| 5 | REVEAL 2 | Second blind spot (escalate tension) | normal |
| 6 | CONNECTION | Link to viewer's daily life (Vietnamese context) | normal |
| 7 | REVEAL 3 | Deepest blind spot (challenge fundamental assumption) | slow |
| 8 | TRADE-OFF | What the book DOES get right. End on positive note | slow |
| 9 | CTA | Engagement hook — soft, inviting, not pushy | fast |

**Emotional Arc**: Curiosity → doubt → recognition → tension → nuanced understanding → respect → action

**Example**: "Điều mà Atomic Habits không nói với bạn" — 37x isn't real, 2-minute rule can backfire, systems need goals. But identity shift is genuinely powerful.

**Vietnamese Notes**: Vietnamese audience rất quen với video tóm tắt sách → contrarian angle tạo surprise. Tone tôn trọng, không cynical — khán giả VN 20-35 yêu self-improvement, muốn nuance chứ không muốn bị phủ nhận.

## Template 2: Hidden Connection

**Trigger**: 2 cuốn sách có overlap bất ngờ — đồng ý hoặc mâu thuẫn trên cùng 1 topic.

**Vault requirement**: 1+ entry in connections/agreements.md hoặc connections/contradictions.md linking 2 books.

**Scene Structure**:

| # | Label | Purpose | Pace |
| - | ----- | ------- | ---- |
| 1 | HOOK | "2 cuốn sách này trông khác nhau hoàn toàn. Nhưng không phải." | slow |
| 2 | BOOK A | Key idea from book A (quick, focused) | normal |
| 3 | BOOK B | Key idea from book B (quick, focused) | normal |
| 4 | COLLISION | The surprising agreement/disagreement nobody sees | slow |
| 5 | EVIDENCE | Why this connection matters — real consequences | normal |
| 6 | SYNTHESIS | The deeper truth neither book reaches alone | normal |
| 7 | CTA | "Bạn đứng về phía nào?" — viewer engagement | fast |

**Emotional Arc**: Intrigue → familiarity → surprise → "oh!" moment → deeper understanding → personal stance

**Example**: Atomic Habits + Deep Work — Clear says environment shapes habits, Newport says environment should be crafted for deep focus. Both agree on environmental control but disagree on spontaneity vs structure.

**Vietnamese Notes**: Format so sánh rất phù hợp YouTube VN (thường là "A vs B"). Nhưng Bookie đi sâu hơn: không chỉ so sánh mà tìm insight mới từ intersection.

## Template 3: Meta-Pattern

**Trigger**: 3+ cuốn sách cùng topic, tất cả đều miss 1 điều.

**Vault requirement**: 3+ entries in a single concepts/ file from different books with an identifiable common gap.

**Scene Structure**:

| # | Label | Purpose | Pace |
| - | ----- | ------- | ---- |
| 1 | HOOK | "Mình đã đọc N cuốn sách về [topic]. Đây là điều KHÔNG CUỐN NÀO nói." | slow |
| 2 | SURVEY 1 | Book 1's position (quick) | fast |
| 3 | SURVEY 2 | Book 2's position (quick) | fast |
| 4 | SURVEY 3 | Book 3's position (quick) | fast |
| 5 | PATTERN | What they ALL agree on — the consensus | normal |
| 6 | BLIND SPOT | What they ALL miss — the gap nobody addresses | slow |
| 7 | THESIS | The unsaid truth from reading them together | normal |
| 8 | CTA | "Cuốn nào nên đọc tiếp?" — community input | fast |

**Emotional Arc**: Authority → rapid-fire familiarity → "hmm interesting" → surprise revelation → new perspective → participation

**Example**: After covering 3+ books on habits/productivity — "Every productivity book assumes you know what you want. None of them addresses the question: what if you're optimizing for the wrong life?"

**Vietnamese Notes**: Format "compilation" rất mạnh trên YouTube VN. 3+ books = Bookie authority. Gap revelation = unique value no single-book channel can provide.

## Template 4: Author Portrait

**Trigger**: Tác giả có background phong phú (bio, controversy, nhiều sách).

**Vault requirement**: authors/ file with substantial content. 2+ books by same author in library.md, hoặc rich biographical research.

**Scene Structure**:

| # | Label | Purpose | Pace |
| - | ----- | ------- | ---- |
| 1 | HOOK | Surprising personal fact about the author | slow |
| 2 | ORIGIN | What shaped their thinking — childhood, pivotal moment | normal |
| 3 | THESIS | Their key contribution to the world | normal |
| 4 | EVIDENCE | Where they're right — strongest proof | normal |
| 5 | CRITICISM | Where they're wrong — what critics say | normal |
| 6 | TWIST | Something most people don't know about them | slow |
| 7 | CTA | "Tác giả nào nên explore tiếp?" | fast |

**Emotional Arc**: Fascination → empathy → understanding → agreement → doubt → surprise → curiosity

**Example**: James Clear portrait — from near-death baseball injury to building a habits empire. But his 37x formula oversimplifies. And his system doesn't account for direction.

**Vietnamese Notes**: Vietnamese audience yêu human stories. Author portrait = parasocial connection. Khi khán giả "biết" tác giả, họ muốn xem EVERY video về tác giả đó → series loyalty.

## Template 5: The Tension

**Trigger**: 2 tác giả có triết lý đối lập căn bản.

**Vault requirement**: High-tension entry in connections/contradictions.md with clear author-vs-author philosophical clash.

**Scene Structure**:

| # | Label | Purpose | Pace |
| - | ----- | ------- | ---- |
| 1 | HOOK | "[Author A] sẽ ghét [Author B]. Đây là lý do." | slow |
| 2 | POSITION A | Author A's core argument — steel-manned, fair | normal |
| 3 | EVIDENCE A | Where A is right — strongest case | normal |
| 4 | POSITION B | Author B's core argument — steel-manned, fair | normal |
| 5 | EVIDENCE B | Where B is right — strongest case | normal |
| 6 | THE CLASH | The specific point where they can't both be right | slow |
| 7 | SYNTHESIS | Is one right? Both? Neither? The truth is messier than either admits | normal |
| 8 | CTA | "Bạn đứng về phía nào?" — polarizing engagement | fast |

**Emotional Arc**: Provocation → respect for A → respect for B → tension → "both make sense but..." → nuanced resolution → personal choice

**Example**: James Clear vs Peter Thiel — "Systems win" vs "Bold goals win." After covering both books, Bookie can produce a Tension video that neither author's fans would expect.

**Vietnamese Notes**: Debate format = high engagement trên YouTube (comments chiến). Steel-man cả 2 phía → Bookie = fair referee, không thiên vị. Audience picks sides → repeat views + shares.
